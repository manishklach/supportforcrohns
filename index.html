Join Us Meet Anya
Founder's Message

Built from lived experience

Hello! I am Anya, a senior at Saint Francis High School. Autoimmune conditions can be extremely difficult to manage, and Crohn's Disease is one of them. Flare-ups can be hard to handle, so getting the right information early matters.

I was diagnosed with Crohn's Disease at age 16 and had trouble finding age-appropriate information. That experience is why I started Support For Crohn's. Through this nonprofit, I want to bring together podcasts, support groups, information sessions, and practical guidance that helps people feel less alone.

  • Learn about experiences with autoimmune diseases
  • Learn about recent technologies and medical advancements in Crohn's Disease
  • Learn from medical professionals
  • Listen to what the experts have to say

What We Want

Our Mission

At Support For Crohn's, we are committed to improving the lives of those affected by Crohn's Disease. We provide a safe space for individuals to share their experiences, connect with others, and access vital information and support. Our goal is to reduce stigma, advocate for policy changes, and ensure more complete care and support for everyone affected.
