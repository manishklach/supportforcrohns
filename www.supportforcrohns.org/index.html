Founder’s Message:
Hello! I am Anya , a senior at Saint Francis High School. Autoimmune conditions can be extremely difficult to manage. Crohn’s Disease is one such autoimmune disease. Flare-ups due to Crohn’s Disease can be hard to manage and hence it is important to get the right information to prevent such flare-ups.

I was diagnosed with Crohn’s Disease at the age of 16, and felt that I had a lot of trouble finding age appropriate information about it . This is why I started Support For Crohn’s.

I believe that everyone with Crohn’s Disease or any other autoimmune disease should get the support and information they need to ensure they do anything in their capacity to reduce these instances. Through this nonprofit, I hope to compile podcasts, support groups, information sessions, and eventually create an AI Chatbot trained to answer your questions about Crohn’s Disease

  • Learn about recent technologies and medical advancements in Crohn’s Disease
  • Learn from Medical Professionals

What we want

Our Mission

At Support For Crohn’s, we are committed to improving the lives of those affected by Crohn’s Disease. We provide a safe space for individuals to share their experiences, connect with others, and access vital information and support. Our goal is to reduce the stigma of Crohn’s Disease, advocate for policy changes, and ensure comprehensive care and support for everyone affected.
